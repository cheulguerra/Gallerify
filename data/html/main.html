Quick Search Tips & Tricks

- Typing any combination of letters will search for images with URL that contains that combination.

- Typing a particular image type shows ony the images of that format.

- Quick Search can filter icons, wallpapers and pixes using the following commands: "show icons", "show wallpapers" and "show px".

- Specify the exact image dimensions.

- Typing "from", followed by a number, filters all the images that have width and height, both larger or equal to that number.

- Typing "more than" or "bigger than" followed by a number, filters all the images that have width and height larger but not equal to that number.

- Typing "to" followed by a number filters all the images that have width and height, both less or equal to that number.

- Typing "less than" or "smaller than" followed by a number filters all the images that have width and height smaller but not equal to that number.

- And of course, combining "from" and "to" works just as well.
